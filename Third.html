Where to stay?

Remeber safety always comes first

Always make sure the place you are staying is safe by checking reviews and messageing those who have stayed there before. Consider your safety before anything else. Then make sure that all of your accomidation needs are met.

What is the budget?

For higher budgets hotels or renting homes could be great options which allow for complete freedom and privacy.

For lower budgets consider staying with a host family, hostel, or program in the desired country. This could vastly lower expenses. Some great resources for this are

  • https://www.workaway.info
  • https://www.hostelworld.com
